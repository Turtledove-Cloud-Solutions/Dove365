The Problem

Sound familiar?

Most small businesses grow faster than their systems. What worked at 5 people breaks at 15. Here's what we hear every week.

01

"Everything lives in someone's head — or inbox"

Client details, follow-up dates, job statuses, commitments — scattered across email, calls, and individual spreadsheets. When someone's off, things get missed.

02

"We can't see what's happening across the business"

No single view of your pipeline, active projects, or team workload. Decisions get made on gut feel rather than data, because the data's too hard to pull together.

03

"We keep reinventing the wheel"

Every new client, project or process starts from scratch. No templates, no consistent approach, no way to onboard new staff without a week of handholding.
