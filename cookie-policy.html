What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They can help a site work correctly, remember choices, understand usage, or support embedded third-party services.

How We Use Cookies

Dove365 uses Cookiebot to manage cookie consent. Depending on the services active on the website, cookies may be used for:

  • Necessary purposes: to support basic website operation, security, consent storage, and page functionality.
  • Preference purposes: to remember choices that improve your browsing experience.
  • Statistics purposes: to understand how visitors use the site, where analytics are enabled and consented to.
  • Marketing purposes: to support marketing or embedded third-party content, where enabled and consented to.
